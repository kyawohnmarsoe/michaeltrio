How tight should a ring be?

Proper ring size will fit comfortably snug, but will resist just a bit when slid over the knuckle. It should take 2-3 seconds to slide it over the knuckle and this is normal, especially with men who typically have larger knuckles. If you size your ring to your knuckle, it will be too big on your finger.

Diamond Upgrade Programme

Can I upgrade any diamond?

Any Certified Earth Natural Diamonds purchased directly from Michael Trio is eligible for the Diamond Upgrade Programme.

How do I determine the price of my new diamond?

Your new certified replacement diamond will have to be of at least 2X or greater in value. For example, a $5,000 diamond can be upgraded to a diamond that costs $10,000. The original diamond that is traded in must be in the original condition, accompanied by the original laboratory grading report. Otherwise, customers will bear the cost of a replacement report.

Can I reuse my ring setting?

Definitely yes, if your diamond is set in a Michael Trio setting, and is in reusable condition, we will be able to set your new diamond back onto the original ring setting. If the new diamond does not fit, you may choose to modify it at an additional cost. For settings that are not from Michael Trio, the diamond must be exchanged without the setting. If you wish to trade in your Michael Trio setting, any possible credit exchange will be determined on a case-by-case basis.

Is the Michael Trio Diamond Upgrade Programme available internationally?

Yes, it is. Please contact our Customer Service about the specifics of international returns.

Diamond Price Match Programme

What is the Michael Trio Diamond Price Match Programme?

Michael Trio strives to provide our customers with the confidence that you are paying the best price for your diamond. Found a comparable diamond at a lower price? Call us at +65 6299 0110 and we will honor the price as long as it fulfills our Diamond Price Match criteria here.

What if I found a comparable diamond at a lower price after my purchase from Michael Trio?

The Diamond Price Match Programme only applies before you purchase your Michael Trio diamond. If, for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase from Michael Trio, you may choose to return your diamond within 14-days from the purchase date.

What is considered a comparable diamond?

A comparable diamond must possess the same characteristics (cut, colour, clarity, carat, and fluorescence) as the Michael Trio diamond to which you are comparing. Michael Trio reserves the right to review your request and decide, in sole discretion, whether the diamond is comparable.

Can the comparable diamond be from any diamond retailer?

We accept price comparisons from all local retail competitors who are authorised Singapore-based diamond retailers in order to ensure ethical sourcing. We will also price match diamonds sold by major online retailers internationally. The diamond in comparison must be in stock and available for purchase at a lower price at the time of your request.

How do I request a price match from Michael Trio?

You may do so by visiting any of our showrooms, contact our customer service hotline at +65 6299 0110 , or email to us at service@michaeltrio.com.

How do I show "proof" of the lower price?

You will be required to provide a copy of the diamond's GIA grading report (issued within the last year) together with proof of the lower price offered.

What if the comparable diamond is no longer in stock?

The lower-priced diamond has to be in stock and available for purchase at the time of your request. Expired offers will not be eligible for price comparison.

Conflict-Free Diamonds

What are conflict-free diamonds?

Conflict-free diamonds are gems that were not obtained due to human rights abuses, child labor, violence, or environmental degradation.

Are your diamonds conflict-free?

At Michael Trio, all diamonds are conflict-free. This is aligned with our brand value of only sourcing from suppliers who meet our internal requirements.

  1. We only purchase polished diamonds from professional members of the diamond trade.
