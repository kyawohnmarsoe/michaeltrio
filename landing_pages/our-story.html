Our Story

At Michael Trio, we confidently provides

1876

Our founding father, Michael, began manufacturing jewellery in 1987. As a family growing in this industry, we wanted to honour his legacy by creating a company that embraced our family values.

2013

We started Michael Trio, Michael being our tribute to our late father, and Trio to describe the three close-knit brothers. We Established Our First show Room in Kallang within the Headquarters.

2014

Opened our first flag ship showroom at Tanjong Pagar just 5 min walk away from the MRT alowing our customer easy access to the show and also bring in more design to show case.

2019

Open 2nd Stall in Jem for our customer in the west.

2021
