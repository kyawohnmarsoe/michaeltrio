Custom-Made Process

1

Consultation

Arrange for an appointment virtually or at any of our 3 showrooms. Feel free to bring along any materials that will help us better understand your ideal design. Need some help in your ideation process? Fret not, as we have a collection of sample products at our showrooms that might just give you some inspiration

2

Design

Upon confirmation of the design, a deposit will be required and our designer will begin sketching the design in accordance with your preference. Once completed, a sketch of your customised item will be shared for final approval.

3

Casting and Setting

Once the design is approved, production of your customised jewellery will commence and all you have to do is sit back and relax!
